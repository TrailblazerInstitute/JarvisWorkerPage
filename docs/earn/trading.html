Revenue / Service 3 · Trading

Why keep trading separate?

As soon as an agent touches trading, system risk rises sharply: capital safety, permission drift, strategy drift, market impact, and compliance responsibility all become harder problems.

So if this page grows, the first principle will not be “more aggressive returns”. It will be “controllable, auditable, and accountable behavior”.

Topics this module would need to cover

  • Budget gate: maximum drawdown, transaction caps, period caps, category limits, and risk budgets.
  • Permission gate: approved interfaces, read-only or paper trading by default, human confirmation for sensitive actions.
  • Accountability gate: strategy versions, data versions, order rationale, logs, and post-trade review.
  • Compliance: jurisdictional limits, disclosure duties, AML / anti-fraud boundaries, and responsibility assignment.
