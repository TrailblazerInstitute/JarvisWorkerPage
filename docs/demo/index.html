        What is this demo really trying to show?

        • Not payment power: the point is not “payment succeeded”, but “why should this spend be allowed at all?”
        • Not free-form consumption: every request passes through budget, permission, and risk checks.
        • Not black-box judgment: the system shows which worker is acting and why the request was approved, rejected, or frozen.
        • Not only concepts: every evaluation produces a traceable ledger entry.
        Note: the thresholds, allowlists, and risk rules here are example policies for demonstration only. Real deployment would need to reflect compliance rules, budget size, task risk, and human approval or multi-signature requirements.
